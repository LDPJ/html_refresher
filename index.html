Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest...

Ingredients
  • 2 eggs
  • i pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes.
  5. Eat and enjoy!
